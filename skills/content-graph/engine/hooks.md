# engine/hooks.md – Hook-Bibliothek

Hooks sind der wichtigste Teil jedes Posts. Erster Satz = alles.

## Hook-Kategorien

### 1. Contrarian Hook
"Everyone does X. I do Y. Here's why I'm outperforming."
"The advice everyone gives about [topic] is wrong."
"Stop [common thing]. It's killing your [result]."

### 2. Number Hook
"I made $X in Y days using this exact system:"
"X things I wish I knew before [event]:"
"After testing Y strategies, only these X work:"

### 3. Pain-Point Hook
"You're losing money because [specific reason]."
"Most people never reach [goal] because of this one mistake:"
"Why your [thing] isn't growing (and it's not what you think):"

### 4. Curiosity Gap Hook
"The reason I deleted my [tool] account (and what I use instead):"
"I tried [trendy thing] for 30 days. Here's the honest result:"
"What nobody tells you about [popular topic]:"

### 5. Story Hook
"6 months ago I was [bad situation]. Today I [good situation]. Thread:"
"I almost gave up on [thing] until I discovered this:"

## Hooks nach Platform
- **X**: Alle Typen – Contrarian performen am besten
- **LinkedIn**: Story Hook + Pain-Point
- **IG Slide 1**: Number + Contrarian (großer Text)
- **TikTok**: Pain-Point + Curiosity (verbal, erste 3s)
- **YouTube Titel**: Number + Contrarian
- **Newsletter Betreff**: Curiosity Gap + Number

## Anti-Patterns (nie benutzen)
- "In this post I will..."
- "Hi everyone!"
- "Just wanted to share..."
- Passive Konstruktionen als Opener
